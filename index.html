HOW TO ORDER

SEND AN EMAIL with these information:
- Your Name/Alias:
- Art Request: the technical details like lineart, flat color, number of characters etc.
- References: images or text with physical description of you character (SHORT description, I will not read pages from books or entire BG stories)
- I Want My Character to Look: use adjectives, like pretty, cute, cool, serious, etc.
- Specific Details: preferred color, atmosphere etc.


-I will reply and confirm your order.
-I'll ask for half the payment before I start working. In case of a queue, I'll ask half payment as deposit to book your place in the queue.
-I will send you up to 3 sketches to chose from, you can specify at this stage if you want a specific pose
-I will show you progresses and you will be able to ask for changes, but only during the sketching phase. After that major changes will be charged.
-I'll ask for the complete payment before sending you the final work.
-I will send the high-resolution finished work through e-mail or We Transfer Link
